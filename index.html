About

Hi I'm Victoria

I'm a self taught artist who has been doing calligraphy and art for almost 10 years. My work has been used by dozens of local businesses and events all over New England. I specialize in hand drawn signs, murals, and custom pieces for weddings and events. I love working with clients to create something unique and special for their business or event. I am happy to work with you to create something that is perfect for your needs. See more of my work on Instagram!

Services

My most common request fall into three catagories

Signage & Wall Art

Hand Drawn Signs and Murals for your business or home (Menus, Logos, Decor Ect). Signs can be done on almost any surface (Wood, Glass, Chalkboards Ect). I also will do the art right on your wall. Whether you need to highlight an area of your business, or you want to add something to your home, I can help you with that.

Weddings & Events

Looking for Wedding or Event Signage Pieces? I am happy to help with that! I can do seating charts, welcome signs, menu/bar signs, and more. I can also do custom pieces for your event. Signs can be done on almost any surface (Wood, Glass, Chalkboards Ect).

Store Front Window Murals

Hand Drawn Windows for your business or storefront that are unique and designed for your individual needs. Have something you want to advertise? Have a holiday you want to bring attention to? Or do you just want a nice decorated window to draw people in? Window Designs & Lettering is a unique way to catch the eye and bring attention to your business.
